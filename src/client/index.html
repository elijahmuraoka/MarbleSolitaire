How to Play

  1. Click on a marble
  2. Make sure there is a marble in between the one you have clicked and an empty space (You can only move in the X or Y directions, not diagonally)
  3. Jump to the empty space from the selected marble to clear the marble in-between

Clear as many marbles as possible or get a score of 1 to win!
